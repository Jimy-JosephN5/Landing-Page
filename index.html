Neymar da Silva Santos Júnior (born 5 February 1992), known as Neymar, is a Brazilian professional footballer who plays as a forward for Ligue 1 club Paris Saint-Germain and the Brazil national team.

Leonel Messi

An Argentine international, Messi is both his country's highest appearance-maker and their all-time leading goalscorer. At youth level, he won the 2005 FIFA World Youth Championship

Ronaldo

Born and raised in Madeira, Ronaldo began his senior club career playing for Sporting CP, before signing with Manchester United in 2003, aged 18, winning the FA Cup in his first season.

Paulo Dybala

Dybala made his senior international debut for Argentina in 2015 at age 22, and has since earned over 25 caps, including appearing at the 2018 FIFA World Cup and the 2019 Copa América.

Mesut Ozil
